Programs & Services

Delivering results with community ownership and cultural respect.

Education & Youth

From classroom rehabilitation to teacher capacity building and youth leadership, we invest in the next generation of Papua New Guineans.

  • Scholarships for secondary and tertiary education
  • School infrastructure and WASH facilities
  • Teacher training and curriculum support

Health & Wellbeing

We strengthen primary health services through outreach, partnerships, and culturally appropriate health education.

  • Maternal and child health initiatives
  • Mobile clinics and rural outreaches
  • Community health promotion and awareness

Livelihoods & Climate

Supporting climate-smart agriculture, MSMEs, and community-based adaptation to build resilient local economies.

  • Agroforestry and sustainable land use
  • Enterprise training and market access
  • Disaster risk reduction and preparedness
